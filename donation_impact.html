Mother's Matter Centre

Program for Parents of Preschool Youngsters (HIPPY) Canada

The Mothers Matter Centre (MMC) is a not-for-profit, registered charity. It is a virtual, national consortium of organizations dedicated to serving socially isolated and low economic status mothers and their families using our proven mother-to-mother approach.

Partners

We work with partners across Canada who are dedicated to supporting low-income women to improve their lives. Programs include Central Vancouver Island Multicultureal society, SPEC Association for Families and Children, Immigrant Women's Association, Newcomer Family Resource Network, HIPPY for Young Mothers, NiL TU, O Child and Family Services, Houston Link to Learning, Mt. Pleasant Neighbourhood House, Burnaby Family Life, Project Literacy Kelowna, etc.

Achievements

The mothers we support come from all walks of life — refugee, newcomer, Aboriginal and other at-risk mothers are regularly involved in our programs. Since our inception, the Mothers Matter Centre has transformed over 40,000 lives. We helped more than 14,000 mothers gain new skills and abilities essential to strengthening mother-child relationships. This means their children and families were better prepared to get the most from their early school experiences.
